How It Works

Simple three-step
publishing flow.

1

Connect Your TikTok Account

Log in securely via TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 flow. ContentFlow only requests the minimum permissions needed to publish to your own account.

2

Queue Your Videos

Select your pre-produced video files, add captions and hashtags, and configure privacy settings per post. Supports bulk queuing for efficiency.

3

Publish & Track

ContentFlow initiates the upload via TikTok's Content Posting API and polls for completion status — so you know exactly when each video goes live.

🛡️ TikTok Policy Compliant

ContentFlow uses only TikTok's official Content Posting API and operates within TikTok's Developer Terms of Service. All content published through ContentFlow is original, creator-owned content. ContentFlow does not access or manage third-party TikTok accounts, does not scrape data, and does not automate engagement (likes, comments, follows).
