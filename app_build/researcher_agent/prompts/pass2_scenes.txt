HOOK SCENE (always Scene 1, always 3–5 seconds):

Open MID-ACTION or MID-QUESTION, never with an introduction
Use the hook_question from Pass 1 as the narration
Visual MUST be the most dramatic/striking image available
— never plain text on background
Tone: conspiratorial whisper, like you're telling a secret

BODY SCENES (one scene per insight_unit):

Scene narration = truth from insight_unit (not assumption)
Each scene must END by either:
a) Answering the question opened by the previous scene, OR
b) Opening a new question that the next scene will answer

Each scene narration: 10–22 words maximum
Each scene duration: calibrate to TTS audio length + 0.3s
buffer, never hardcode


VERDICT SCENE (second to last, 4–6 seconds):

Deliver the narrative_payoff from Pass 1

Visual: the most conceptually complete image in the sequence
