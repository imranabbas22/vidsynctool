Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026  ·  Effective: January 1, 2026

ContentFlow is a personal video publishing tool used by an independent content creator to publish original videos to their own TikTok account. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Who We Are

ContentFlow ("we", "us", or "our") is an independent creator tool. It is operated by a solo content creator for the purpose of automating the publishing of original, AI-assisted short-form video content to TikTok. ContentFlow is not affiliated with TikTok or ByteDance.

2. Information We Collect

ContentFlow collects the minimum data required to function. This includes:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you connect your TikTok account via TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 login, ContentFlow receives a user access token and basic account identifiers (such as your open ID and username). This is used solely to authenticate your publishing requests.
  • Video Metadata: Captions, hashtags, and privacy settings you configure for each video post.
  • Publish Status Data: Response data from TikTok's Content Posting API (publish IDs, status codes) used to track whether a video published successfully.

ContentFlow does not collect or store sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment details, or location data.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected by ContentFlow is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0
  • Submit video publishing requests to TikTok's Content Posting API on your behalf
  • Track publish status for videos you have queued
  • Display confirmation that a video has been successfully published

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond video publishing functionality.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

ContentFlow does not:

  • Access or store TikTok account passwords
  • Access third-party TikTok accounts
  • Collect your followers, likes, comments, or engagement data
  • Track your browsing behaviour or usage analytics
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Sell any data to any party

5. TikTok API Data Usage

ContentFlow accesses the TikTok platform through TikTok's official Content Posting API under the following scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to retrieve the authenticated creator's available privacy levels before publishing
  • video.publish — to publish video content to the authenticated creator's own TikTok profile
  • video.upload — to upload video files to TikTok's servers as part of the two-step publish flow

All data obtained from the TikTok API is used only in accordance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service.

6. Data Storage & Retention

ContentFlow operates primarily as a local tool. Access tokens and publish logs are stored locally on the device running ContentFlow and are not transmitted to any external server operated by us. Access tokens are stored only as long as needed to complete the publishing session and are not retained beyond their expiry.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. The only external service ContentFlow communicates with is TikTok's official API endpoints at open.tiktokapis.com.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke ContentFlow's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's Connected Apps settings
  • Request deletion of any locally stored data by simply removing ContentFlow from your device
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns about this privacy policy

9. Security

ContentFlow uses TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 authentication system, which means your TikTok password is never shared with or stored by ContentFlow. We follow industry-standard security practices for handling access tokens.

10. Children's Privacy

ContentFlow is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of ContentFlow after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address listed on this website.
